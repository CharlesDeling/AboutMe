Warhammer

My second main hobby is playing a tabletop game called Warhammer 40K with my friends. I have only been into 40K for about a year as of this point, yet it has quickly become one of my favorite things to do. I enjoy aspects of the hobby, from building the models, painting the models, reading the lore, and playing the game itself. I would try to explain the story behind 40k, yet that could easily take multiple pages to explain. However, I definitely recommend checking it out as it has some of the best story writing I have ever read. The tabletop game on the otherhand is a lot easier to explain. In short, you collect and build models that each have unique rules. Each model costs a certain number of points to field and you build up an army depending on how many points are going to be used in a game. For example, an average game is 2,000 points for each person, so if I add up the points cost of every model in my army, they must be less than or equal to 2,000 points. After each person has their armies, they fight each other using the rules that their models have. There are a lot more specifics than that, but this is just a brief description. My favorite aspect of this hobby is painting the miniatures. It's both relaxing and rewarding at the same time. I don't even know how many times I have spent a night listening to music and painting models. It honestly has the same impact that gaming does, it also allows me to disconnect from reality for a bit. It's also really nice to spend a couple of hours painting and have something completed that I painted myself. Plus, it really does make the experience of playing 40K on tabletop a lot more immersive than if I had unpainted models. However, probably the most important part of this hobby for me is that it reignited my creativity. When I was younger, I used to love sketching and painting, but I slowly lost my creativity and passion for it, which I believe to be partially caused by gaming. Yet, painting these miniatures has helped reignited that passion and creativity, which I truely am glad about. Below are some of my favorite models that I have painted up till this point.
